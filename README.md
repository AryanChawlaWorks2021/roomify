# 🏠 Roomify

🔗 **Live Project:**  
https://puter.com/app/roomify-48

Roomify is an AI-powered architectural visualization platform that transforms 2D floor plans into realistic 3D renders. The platform allows users to generate visualizations, compare results, manage projects, and share their architectural designs with a global community.

# 🚀 Features

## 🧠 AI-Powered 2D → 3D Visualization
Transform flat architectural floor plans into realistic **3D renders** using advanced AI-powered image generation.

---

## ☁️ Persistent Media Hosting
All uploaded files and generated renders are **securely stored with permanent public URLs**, ensuring long-term accessibility.

---

## 🗂 Dynamic Project Gallery
A personalized workspace where users can **view, manage, and revisit previous visualizations** with persistent project metadata.

---

## 🔍 Side-by-Side Comparison
Compare the **original architectural sketch and the AI-generated 3D output** interactively to clearly visualize the transformation.

---

## 🌍 Global Community Feed
Explore architectural projects created by other users and **share your own designs with the global community** in a single click.

---

## 🔐 Privacy & Visibility Controls
Control who can access your projects through **public and private visibility settings**, ensuring privacy and security.

---

## 🧾 Ownership & Metadata Tracking
A structured metadata system associates **projects with user accounts**, enabling organized project management and clear ownership tracking.

---

## 📥 Export & Download Tools
Download AI-generated renders easily for **presentations, documentation, or professional workflows**.

---

## 🧩 Scalable Code Architecture
Built with **modular and reusable components**, making the application easier to maintain, extend, and scale.
